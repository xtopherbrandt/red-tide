What Is The Smart Cellar

What if you could add a bottle to your cellar ... without making notes?

What if you could find a bottle to your cellar ... by asking where it is?

What if you could get wine suggestions ... from your cellar?

What if you could move bottles around your cellar ... and still find them?

What if your prized bottles could prove their own authenticity?

What if your prized bottles could prove how well they've been stored?

What if your cellar could tell you when that bottle is ready?

How Does The Smart Cellar Work

In The Smart Cellar, every bottle is uniquely identified by a Smart Cellar tag. Bottles can be tagged at home, by their owner, but will ultimately be tagged at the winery by the producer. This means that you will be able to buy a Smart Cellar tagged bottle and simply tap it into your Smart Cellar.

The Smart Cellar tags are RFID labels containing a serial number unique to every bottle. Your Smart Cellar will have internet connected pods that can both read the RFID label when tapped and display a light beacon to help you find a bottle.

In addition to having a unique serial number, every bottle can also have a set of cryptographic keys that will allow a bottle's identity to be verified and authenticated. This is envisioned as a solution to wine counterfeiting that will be simply enough to allow anyone to determine the authenticity of a bottle, but strong enough to deter wine counterfeiters.

Eventually The Smart Cellar will consist of an eco-system of connected sensors and devices. Sensors will be able to detect environment conditions such as temperature, humidity, light intensity, vibration... and record these storage conditions for every bottle feeling them. Each bottle will collect a history of where it has been kept and the conditions it was kept in providing the owner with valuable provenance data if and when they want to sell. The same data could also be used by The Smart Cellar to tell you when to drink that bottle you've kept for so long.

It doesn't stop there. We're constantly thinking about the problems that we have as wine collectors and how The Smart Cellar can help us solve them. If you have unique problems, questions or ideas for us we want to hear them.
